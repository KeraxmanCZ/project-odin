Egg Sandwich

Just try it

Ingredients

  • Boiled Eggs
  • Fatty Cottage Cheese
  • Carrot
  • Wild Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Toast Bread
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Either chop the eggs coarsely with a knife or, even better, use a potato salad grater.
  2. Mix the cottage cheese, carrot and chopped wild garlic with the eggs
  3. Finally, season the salad with salad vinegar and a pinch of salt. It is best to let the salad sit for at least half an hour.
  4. If you want to fill the salad in sandwiches, we recommend larger slices of toasted bread, which you lightly toast before filling.
  5. The bread then does not get soggy and really lasts.
