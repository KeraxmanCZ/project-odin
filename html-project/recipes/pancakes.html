Pancakes With Spinach

Just try it

Ingredients

  • Chicken meat
  • Spinach
  • Garlic
  • Eidam Cheese
  • Oil
  • cream
  • Onion

Pancakes

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. We prepare the dough for pancakes, fry the onion in oil, cut the meat into cubes, add it to the onion, let it fry. Add the spinach, close the lid so that the spinach dissolves. In the meantime, we will start frying the pancakes.
  2. Pour cream over the chicken mixture, add pressed garlic and season with salt. The mixture must thicken, it is possible to add grated edamame. Put the finished mixture into pancakes and cover with grated cheese.
