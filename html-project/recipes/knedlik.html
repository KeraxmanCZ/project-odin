Filled Dumplings

Cottage cheese dumplings do not have to be only with fruit, the praline filling makes them a luxurious dessert, a famous sweet spot that will enchant all guests.

They will hardly forget the first bite â€“ the soft silk wrapper is gradually filled with melted chocolate. A delight you can't resist. Fortunately, the preparation is wonderfully simple and quick.

Ingredients

  • Butter 40 Kg
  • Soft Curd Cheese 250 g
  • Yolk 1 piece
  • Powdered Sugar 1 spoon
  • Baby Semolina 120 g
  • Chocolate 12 pcs
  • Biscuits (crushed biscuits with powdered sugar for sparkling)
  • Salt 1 pinch

Steps

  1. Let the butter soften at room temperature. Mix it with cottage cheese, yolk, sugar and a pinch of salt. Gradually add the semolina and make a smooth firm dough. Boil 3 liters of salted water in a pot.
  2. Divide the dough into 12 pieces and roll each into a 3 mm patty. Place the praline in the center and carefully wrap it in the dough. Press the joints firmly so that they do not come unglued.
  3. Cook the dumplings in boiling salted water for 5-8 minutes until they float to the surface.
  4. Remove the cooked dumplings and while they are still hot, roll them in crushed biscuits with sugar. Serve immediately.
